# FairValue Transfer Cap Estimator: Pitch Deck

**"Removing Emotion from the Transfer Window"**

## 1. The Problem: The Winner’s Curse
- **Clubs consistently overpay:** Emotional bidding wars and agent-driven scarcity lead to inflated transfer fees.
- **Amortization Trap:** High fees create toxic balance sheets that become impossible to offload if the player underperforms.
- **Regulatory Risk:** PSR/FFP rules now make "overpaying" a sporting catastrophe, not just a financial one.

## 2. The Solution: FairValue
A proprietary "Hard Cap" calculator that uses **Hedonic Pricing Theory** and **Machine Learning** to strip away market noise.

## 3. The Core Technology: "The Busquets Factor"
Our model doesn't just look at goals; it quantifies **Asset Liquidity Risk**:
- **Contract Leverage:** Quantitative tracking of depreciating contract years.
- **Age Decay:** Log-linear modeling of the physical peak window.
- **Structural Injury Index:** Identifying players with recurring availability risks.

## 4. Competitive Advantage
- **Objective Baseline:** Uses 12,500+ historical benchmarks to set a baseline price.
- **Risk Discounting:** Unlike Transfermarkt, which gives a "valuation," we provide a **"Hard Cap"**—the maximum you can pay before the ROI becomes statistically negative.

## 5. Roadmap
- **V1 (Current):** MVP using public Kaggle/Transfermarkt data.
- **V2:** Integration of real-time Opta/StatsBomb performance event data.
- **V3:** Portfolio risk management for entire squad recruitment strategies.
